Number 7

24/08/2025

This was a nice easy one. It only took me 1 hours and 41 minutes to complete and it was really the first warm day of spring, so I could take it easy and not really worry about changing layers. It's nice to go for some smaller walks after doing all of the massive 15+ kilometre ones. The 7 goes from Kingston, to Wellington Station, so it's a bit of a unique take on the classic south-north bus route that I am quite used to at this point.

You get a good view of Wellington from this route, as it's mostly on a ridge until you reach Brooklyn Road, which takes you into the town center.
